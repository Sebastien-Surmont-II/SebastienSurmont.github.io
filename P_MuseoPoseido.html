Museo Poseído

I made this game with a wonderful team of 5 where I was the lead programmer, but I took on more of a central role as the team lead as well. We worked for a Mexican museum called 3 Museos to bring this game to life in which ghosts have taken over the museum and you've gotta suck them all up to bring the museum back to its clean state.

The Process

For this project I had to tackle a variety of issues such as our team having a bad distribution of talent. I was the only programmer who was able to quickly get tools and mechanics programmed, but as nobody else took initiative I was also the one who made the major design decions & managed the team/project.

I managed to succeed in juggling these tasks by making sure I didn't have to micromanage the project, to achieve this I had to let go of the minor decisions within the project & I also installed who I trusted as the best artist as Art Lead. That way I didn't have to worry about art at all as I could trust the art lead's judgement.

Thanks to my teammates' great contrubtions like the amazing sounds and music from our sound artist & cute animations from my friend on the right we were able to deliver a final result that our client was very happy with.
