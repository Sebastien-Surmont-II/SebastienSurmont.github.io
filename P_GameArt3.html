Modular Assets

This project is a collection of modular assets & shaders I made to be able to easily make games look good & build on top of when I need something a bit different.

Village put together with the assets

The Process

I started off making a bunch of fluffy nature assets, so that I could basically fill in any part of the map with vegitation. Once that was done I started plotting out what I would base the assets on, I landed on roman & old italian architecture. I made some building assets & as I refined what assets I needed I also refined the art style to make it what it is now.

The Assets

I made a bunch of handy tools to make it possible to make a whole scene in a matter of an hour instead of days or weeks.
