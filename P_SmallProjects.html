Active Ragdoll Parkour

In this Parkour/Fighting game you play as a drunk guy who got stranded on a rooftop and aquired superpowers. Use those powers and more in order to fight your way through the police to your clothes.

Project Link

The Process

For this project I started off wanting to make a wipeout inspired parkour game with a bunch of obstacles. So I made a style mix between 2D & 3D, but then I added a cube that could attack the player just to itterate and found that to be a lot more fun. So I decided to go into more of a parkour fighting game direction.

Since the fun could be found in fighting I decided to go further with it and allow you to fight a bunch of enemies that move like you & even gave the player overpowered abilities that they could use with a cooldown. I then released it on itch.io where it got a good amount of downloads.
